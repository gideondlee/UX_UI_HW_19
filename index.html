ABOUT ME



Hello, I’m GIDEON,

a product designer engrossed in alleviating user-centered design problems through empathic and innovative design. With a psychology degree from UCSD, I aspire to iron out, improve and fluidify the interactions between a product and its users.

My interests and passions in the human psyche as well as the creative spurred my decision to pursue a career in product design. I immensely enjoy the process of constantly iterating and improving both myself and my projects. I love that design is a continual battle in progression and enhancing oneself.

I am always seeking ways to work on myself and my craft. If I’m not hashing out my design process, you could find me working on music production, perfecting the art of coffee (be it the clean black, pourover drip or a perfect frothy latte), or watching the Lakers or Dodgers attempt at another championship run.

I love meeting new people and learning what inspires them, so please feel free to reach out!
